Meet Kabir Virji



He's the president and founder of the UofT Think Tank


Hi there! My name is Kabir and I'm the president and founder of the UofT Think Tank. I'm a second year Computer Science student at the University of Toronto, and I'm currently a software developer for an e-commerce startup called LemonStand.


When I first started the Think Tank, I had a vision of a better world. Software touches so much of our lives, and by being applying what I know about programming to different fields, I could really make a difference.


I also wanted to learn about new things, outside of Computer Science. This is when the idea of starting a Think Tank first came to me, and due to great team members, it has turned out to be a success.


I'd love to hear from you. You can go to my website to learn more about me, or email me to get in touch. Also, don't forget to apply to the Think Tank!
